# Agile & Scrum

## Core Values (Agile Manifesto)
- Individuals & interactions > Processes & tools
- Working software > Comprehensive documentation
- Customer collaboration > Contract negotiation
- Responding to change > Following a plan

## Scrum Roles
- **Product Owner**: represents stakeholders, owns backlog, prioritizes
- **Scrum Master**: facilitates process, removes blockers, coaches team
- **Development Team**: self-organizing, cross-functional, 3-9 people

## Scrum Events
- **Sprint Planning**: what + how for next sprint (max 8h for 1-month sprint)
- **Daily Standup**: 15 min, what did I do / what will I do / blockers
- **Sprint Review**: demo completed work to stakeholders (max 4h)
- **Retrospective**: inspect & adapt the process (max 3h)

## Sprint Cadence
- Typical: 1-2 weeks (max 1 month)
- Sprint goal: single unifying objective
- Definition of Done: shared quality standard
- Velocity: work completed per sprint (use for forecasting, not comparison)

## Kanban
- Visualize workflow (columns: To Do → In Progress → Review → Done)
- Limit Work in Progress (WIP limits)
- Manage flow, measure cycle time
- Continuous delivery (no fixed sprints)

## Estimation
- Story points (relative sizing, Fibonacci sequence: 1,2,3,5,8,13,21)
- Planning Poker to avoid anchoring bias
- T-shirt sizes (XS, S, M, L, XL)
- Never use points for performance evaluation
