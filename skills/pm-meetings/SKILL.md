# Meeting Facilitation

## Before Meeting
- Is this meeting necessary? Could it be async?
- Define objective: inform, decide, brainstorm, or align?
- Agenda with time allocations, distributed 24h+ in advance
- Invite only essential people (max 8 for decision meetings)
- Pre-read materials shared with agenda

## During Meeting
- Start on time, end on time (timebox each item)
- State objective and agenda at start
- One conversation at a time
- Capture: decisions, action items, open questions
- Parking lot for off-topic items
- Last 5min: summarize decisions and next steps

## After Meeting
- Minutes within 24h: decisions, action items (owner + due date)
- Action items tracked in project management system
- Follow up on overdue items before next meeting

## Meeting Types

### Daily Standup (15min)
- What did I complete? What will I do? Any blockers?
- Don't problem-solve during standup (take offline)

### Sprint Planning (max 4h for 2-week sprint)
- Capacity planning, task breakdown, sprint goal
- Definition of Done reviewed

### Retrospective (max 1.5h)
- What went well? What could improve? What will we try?
- Action items with owners for top 1-3 improvements

### SteerCo / Status (1h)
- Progress vs plan, key metrics, decisions needed
- Executive summary, exception-based reporting

## Async Alternatives
- Status updates: written report, dashboard
- Decisions: RFC document with comment period
- Brainstorming: shared doc, async comments
