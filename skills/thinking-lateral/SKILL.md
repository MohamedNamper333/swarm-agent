# Lateral & Creative Thinking

## Divergent Thinking Techniques

### SCAMPER
- **S**ubstitute — what can I replace?
- **C**ombine — what can I merge?
- **A**dapt — what else is like this?
- **M**odify — what can I change (size, shape, color)?
- **P**ut to other use — what else can this do?
- **E**liminate — what can I remove?
- **R**earrange — what if I reverse the order?

### Reverse Brainstorming
- Instead of "How do I solve X?" ask "How do I CAUSE X?"
- Then reverse the generated ideas

### Random Input
- Pick a random word / image / concept
- Force connections to the problem
- The unrelated concept triggers new associations

### Six Thinking Hats (Edward de Bono)
- **White**: facts and data (neutral)
- **Red**: emotions and intuition
- **Black**: risks and caution (judgment)
- **Yellow**: benefits and optimism
- **Green**: creativity and new ideas
- **Blue**: process and organization (facilitator)

## Idea Evaluation
- Defer judgment during generation (no criticism in brainstorming)
- Quantity over quality in divergent phase
- Converge later: cluster, prioritize, select
- Use impact/effort matrix (2×2 grid)

## Overcoming Mental Blocks
- Constraint relaxation: remove one constraint and see what's possible
- Analogy thinking: how is this like something completely different?
- What would [Einstein/Jobs/Musk/Archimedes] do?
- Imagine you're from 100 years in the future looking back
