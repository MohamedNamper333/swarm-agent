# Change Management

## Change Control Process
1. **Submit**: change request with description, justification, impact
2. **Assess**: evaluate impact on scope, schedule, cost, quality, risk
3. **Review**: Change Control Board (PM, sponsor, stakeholders)
4. **Decide**: approve, reject, defer, or modify
5. **Update**: update baselines, plan, communicate decision

## Change Request Template
- Requestor, date, priority (critical/high/medium/low)
- Description of change
- Business justification
- Impact assessment (scope, schedule, cost, quality, risk)
- Alternatives considered

## Impact Assessment
| Dimension | Current | After Change | Delta |
|---|---|---|---|
| Schedule | 12 weeks | 15 weeks | +3 weeks |
| Cost | $500K | $620K | +$120K |
| Quality | — | — | No change |
| Risk | Medium | High | New dependency |

## Types of Change
- **Scope creep**: uncontrolled additions (prevent via strict change control)
- **Gold plating**: team adds features not requested (educate team)
- **Corrective**: bring performance back in line with plan
- **Preventive**: avoid potential future deviation

## Baseline Management
- Schedule, cost, and scope baselines
- Only change through approved change requests
- Version history of all changes
- Audit trail of who approved what, when
