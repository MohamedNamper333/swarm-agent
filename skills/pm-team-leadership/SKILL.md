# Team Leadership

## Team Development (Tuckman)
1. **Forming**: introduce, set expectations, define roles
2. **Storming**: address conflict, establish norms, build trust
3. **Norming**: align on processes, build cohesion
4. **Performing**: high productivity, autonomous, self-organizing
5. **Adjourning**: celebrate, retrospective, knowledge transfer

## Motivation
- **Autonomy**: give ownership and decision authority
- **Mastery**: provide learning and growth opportunities
- **Purpose**: connect work to business impact
- Recognition: public praise, peer awards, career progression

## Conflict Resolution
1. **Identify**: surface conflict early, don't ignore
2. **Listen**: hear both sides without judgment
3. **Focus on interests**: not positions (what do they really need?)
4. **Find common ground**: shared goals, mutual benefits
5. **Agree on action**: specific steps, owner, timeline
6. **Follow up**: check within a week

## Coaching
- **GROW Model**: Goal → Reality → Options → Will
- Ask questions, don't give answers
- Focus on strengths, not just fixing weaknesses
- Create growth plans with measurable objectives

## Delegation
- Match task complexity to skill level
- Define: outcome, authority level, support, checkpoints
- Don't delegate: performance reviews, confidential issues, team culture
- Use delegation to stretch and grow team members

## Performance Management
- Clear expectations from day one
- Regular 1:1s (weekly 30min)
- Real-time feedback (don't save for reviews)
- Address underperformance early with improvement plans
